AI & Enterprise UX

Designing for trust in complex environments. I specialize in turning "black box" algorithms into transparent, user-centric tools. At NiCE | Cognigy and Ultimate.ai, I designed interfaces that helped global enterprises build, test, and analyze AI agents with confidence. My focus is on explainability, data visualization, and giving humans control over automation.

0→1 Product Strategy

From vague concepts to validated MVPs. I thrive in ambiguity. From leading stakeholder workshops to defining product roadmaps, I bridge the gap between business viability and user needs. My strategic approach to product discovery has driven successful 0→1 launches and directly contributed to the value of products acquired by industry leaders like Zendesk and Sendinblue (Brevo).

Systems & Scalability

Building frameworks that grow with the business. Designing for B2B SaaS means designing for scale. Whether it's crafting the highly flexible "Chameleon" design system for Ultimate’s native chat widget, or simplifying the complex architecture of Brevo's core email marketing editor, I build robust systems that empower users and reduce engineering debt.

T-Shaped Execution

Bridging the gap between design and engineering. With over 14 years of experience and a strong background in frontend development, I don't just draw wireframes. I understand technical constraints and collaborate closely with developers. From high-fidelity prototyping and custom UI illustrations to logic flows, I ensure that strategic concepts are flawlessly executed in production.
